# ServiceHive — AutoStream SaaS Product

## Company Overview

AutoStream is a next-generation SaaS platform by **ServiceHive** that enables content creators,
marketers, and businesses to automate, schedule, and optimize their live-streaming workflows
across multiple social media platforms simultaneously. With AutoStream, you go live everywhere
in one click — no technical setup, no juggling multiple tools.

## Key Features

### Multi-Platform Streaming
Stream to multiple platforms in a single click. No need to manage separate streams or tools.
Set it up once, go live everywhere instantly.

### AI-Powered Stream Optimization
AutoStream uses machine learning to automatically adjust bitrate, resolution, and encoding
settings based on your internet connection and platform requirements in real time.
No technical knowledge required.

### Real-Time Analytics Dashboard
Monitor your live viewer count, engagement rate, and chat activity across all platforms
from a single unified dashboard. See which platform drives the most engagement.

### Automated Scheduling
Schedule streams in advance. AutoStream will automatically start your stream at the
scheduled time, even if you're not at your computer. Ideal for global audiences.

### Custom Overlays and Branding
Add your logo, lower-thirds, alerts, and custom graphics with a drag-and-drop editor.
No design skills needed. Templates included.

### Webhook and API Integrations (Pro)
Integrate with your existing tools — CRMs, Slack, Zapier, Notion — using webhooks
and a REST API. Automate your entire content workflow.

### Stream Recording & Export
All streams are automatically recorded in the cloud.
- **Export formats**: MP4 (H.264), MP4 (H.265/HEVC), MOV, WebM
- **Resolution**: up to 4K (Pro) or 720p (Basic)
- **Processing speed**: recordings are available within **5–10 minutes** of stream end
- Download from your dashboard or share a direct link

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time?**
Yes. Upgrade anytime from your dashboard. You'll be charged the prorated difference.

**Does AutoStream work on mobile?**
AutoStream has a web dashboard accessible on all devices. iOS and Android apps are on
the roadmap for Q3 2025.

**Is there a limit on stream duration for the Basic plan?**
Yes. Basic users have a combined limit of 10 hours/month. Pro users have unlimited hours.

**What happens if my internet drops during a stream?**
AutoStream auto-reconnects within 30 seconds. If reconnection fails, the stream ends and
your recording (if enabled) is saved automatically.

**Do you offer discounts for annual billing?**
Yes — 20% off both plans:
- Basic Annual: ~$23/month (billed as $276/year)
- Pro Annual: ~$63/month (billed as $756/year)

**What export formats are supported for stream recordings?**
Recordings export as MP4 (H.264), MP4 (H.265/HEVC), MOV, and WebM.
4K exports are available on the Pro plan. Basic exports up to 720p.

**How fast are recordings available after a stream ends?**
Recordings are processed and ready to download within **5–10 minutes** of your stream ending.
You'll get an email notification when your recording is ready.

**Can I edit my stream after it goes live?**
AutoStream does not offer in-app video editing. However, you can download your recording
in any supported format and edit it in your preferred software (Premiere, Final Cut, DaVinci).
Clip extraction and chapter markers are on the Pro roadmap.

**How does AutoStream compare to streaming manually to each platform?**
Manual multi-platform streaming typically takes 30–60 minutes of setup per session and
requires technical knowledge of RTMP, encoders, and bitrate settings. AutoStream reduces
this to a single click and saves creators 3–5 hours per week on average.

**Can multiple team members use one account?**
Basic includes 1 seat. Pro includes up to 5 seats. Additional seats can be added
on the Pro plan for $10/seat/month.

**Is Twitch streaming available on Basic?**
No. Twitch streaming is a Pro-only feature. Basic supports YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn,
Twitter/X, TikTok, and Facebook Live.
